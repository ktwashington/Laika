<h2>🌌 Inspiration</h2>

Laika is named after Laika, the first dog to orbit Earth aboard Sputnik 2 in 1957. Just as Laika paved the way for human space exploration, this project aims to help students navigate their academic journeys with structure, resilience, and thoughtful planning. College (and high school) can sometimes feel like being launched into space - overwhelming deadlines, competing priorities, and limited time. Laika helps students stay grounded while reaching their goals.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

<h2>🎯 Mission</h2>

Laika is a mindful assignment schedule generator that creates balanced, realistic study plans based on:

📅 Assignment deadlines

⏳ Estimated workload

🧠 Healthy pacing principles

⚖️ Burnout prevention

Instead of cramming or procrastinating, students receive a structured plan that distributes work intelligently over time.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

<h2>✨ Features</h2>
Automated Schedule Generation: Generates a day-by-day plan from inputted deadlines and workload estimates.

Mindful Workload Distribution: Avoids overloading single days and spreads work evenly across available time.

Deadline Prioritization: Assignments are weighted and scheduled according to urgency.

Flexible Inputs: Supports multiple assignments with different due dates and time estimates.

Simplicity First: Clean, straightforward interface focused on clarity and usability.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

<h2>🧠 How It Works</h2>
<h3>The user inputs:</h3>

Assignment name

Due date

Estimated hours required

<h3>Laika:</h3>

Calculates available time before each deadline

Distributes work incrementally across days

Ensures balanced daily workload

<h3>The user receives:</h3>

A structured daily schedule

Clear workload expectations

Reduced last-minute stress
